Shield of Carceri | 2nd-level abjuration
  Casting Time: 1 action
  Range: Self
  Components: V, S, M (a small marble)
  Duration: 1 hour
    A swarm of floating orbs surround you, granting you power and protection. Choose one: Acid, Cold, Fire, Force, Necrotic, or Poison. You gain 6 temporary hit points for the duration. If a creature hits you with a melee attack while you have these hit points, the creature takes 6 damage of the chosen element.
     When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3nd level or higher, both the temporary hit points and the chosen elemental damage increase by 6 for each slot.
    Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock
